# Company Scenario

## Business Overview
Crestview Manufacturing Services is a 100-user company using Microsoft 365 for email, collaboration, document sharing, and identity-driven access.

## Departments
- HR
- Finance
- Sales
- Operations
- IT
- Executive

## Business Problem
The company does not have a standardized joiner-mover-leaver process. New hires are onboarded manually, department changes leave users with old access, and offboarding depends too heavily on memory and manual cleanup.

## Business Risks
- New hires do not receive the right access on time
- Movers retain access from prior roles
- Leavers may keep access longer than intended
- Licenses stay assigned unnecessarily
- Shared resources and collaboration spaces are not cleaned up consistently
- Audit evidence is weak or missing

## Success Criteria
- Joiners receive standard access by department and role
- Movers lose old access and gain only the new access they need
- Leavers are blocked, de-licensed, and removed from access paths in a defined order
- Every major lifecycle action produces validation evidence
