Project 06 Batch 6 — Risk Policy Separation Decision Note

Purpose:
Document why sign-in risk and user risk are planned as separate Conditional Access policies.

Sign-in risk policy:
CA004-SignIn-Risk-MFA

User risk policy:
CA005-User-Risk-Password-Change

Decision:
Sign-in risk and user risk will not be combined into the same Conditional Access policy.

Reason:
Sign-in risk evaluates the risk of a specific authentication attempt.
User risk evaluates the probability that the user account itself is compromised.

Planned response:
- Sign-in risk medium/high: require MFA
- User risk high: require password change

Safety decision:
No risk-based Conditional Access policy is enforced in Batch 6.
Risk policies are planned and documented only.
