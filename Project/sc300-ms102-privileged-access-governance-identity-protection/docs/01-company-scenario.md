# Company Scenario

## Business Overview
Northstar Advisory Group is a 250-user company using Microsoft 365 for productivity, collaboration, and identity-driven access.

## Departments
- Executive
- Finance
- HR
- Sales
- Operations
- IT
- Security

## Business Problem
The company has grown quickly and now has too much standing administrative privilege, inconsistent admin-role assignment, and limited structure for responding to risky sign-ins or compromised identities.

## Business Risks
- admins hold more access than they need every day
- privileged accounts create a larger blast radius if compromised
- high-risk sign-ins may not be handled consistently
- identity incidents take longer to contain
- privileged access evidence is weak or incomplete

## Success Criteria
- privileged roles are mapped to clear job functions
- standing privilege is reduced through eligible access design
- risk-based identity controls are planned and validated
- pilot users are clearly scoped and licensed correctly
- evidence exists for each major privileged-access and identity-protection control
