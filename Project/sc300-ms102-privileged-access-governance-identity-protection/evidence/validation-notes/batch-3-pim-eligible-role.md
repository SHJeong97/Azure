# Batch 3 Validation - PIM Eligible Role and Activation

## Build Scope
This batch enabled Microsoft Entra role management through Privileged Identity Management, configured a PIM-managed eligible assignment for User Administrator, and validated just-in-time activation.

## Business Outcome
The company now has a pilot model that reduces standing privileged access and replaces it with controlled, time-bound activation.

## Validation Results
- PIM for Microsoft Entra roles was used for the pilot role model
- Ethan Walker was assigned User Administrator as an eligible role
- Activation settings required stronger control at elevation time
- Ethan successfully activated the role through My roles
- The activation was validated as time-bound rather than permanently active
