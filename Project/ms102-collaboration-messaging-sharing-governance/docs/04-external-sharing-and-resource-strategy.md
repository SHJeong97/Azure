# External Sharing and Resource Strategy

## Objective
The company needs a clear collaboration model for internal department resources and controlled external sharing.

## Resource Types
- shared mailboxes
- department Teams
- department SharePoint sites

## External Sharing Strategy
The project will use a controlled pilot approach for external collaboration rather than tenant-wide broad sharing.

## Business Logic
A collaboration environment is not well governed if mail, files, and Teams are created without a clear rule for ownership, access, and external participation.
