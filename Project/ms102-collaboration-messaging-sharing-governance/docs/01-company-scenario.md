# Company Scenario

## Business Overview
Summit Advisory Services is a 200-user company using Microsoft 365 for email, file collaboration, team communication, and external business communication.

## Departments
- Executive
- HR
- Finance
- Sales
- Operations
- IT

## Business Problem
The company has grown quickly and now has inconsistent mailbox delegation, unclear shared resource ownership, weak external sharing controls, and uneven collaboration governance across Exchange, Teams, and SharePoint.

## Business Risks
- shared mailboxes are granted manually without clear standards
- external sharing may be broader than intended
- collaboration spaces are not aligned to business purpose
- delegated access is hard to review
- Teams, SharePoint, and Exchange administration is inconsistent across departments

## Success Criteria
- shared mailboxes and collaboration spaces are created from a clear standard
- administrative roles are separated by Microsoft 365 workload
- external sharing is defined and validated
- department resources are mapped intentionally
- evidence exists for each major Microsoft 365 admin control
