# Teams, SharePoint, and Distribution Governance

## Business Objective
Department collaboration resources must be created from a standard so ownership, membership, and purpose are clear across Microsoft Teams, SharePoint Online, and Exchange Online.

## What This Batch Builds
- Department Teams
- Department SharePoint communication sites
- Distribution group membership baseline

## Design Decisions
- Teams are created as private department collaboration spaces
- SharePoint communication sites are created separately for department publishing and document access validation
- Distribution group ownership and membership are set intentionally
- External sharing is deferred to the next batch

## Business Value
This creates a repeatable Microsoft 365 collaboration model where resource ownership and access are visible and easier to govern.
