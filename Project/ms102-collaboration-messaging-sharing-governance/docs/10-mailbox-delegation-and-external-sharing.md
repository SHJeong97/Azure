# Mailbox Delegation and External Sharing

## Business Objective
The company needs delegated mailbox access and external collaboration to follow a controlled model instead of being handled informally.

## What This Batch Builds
- Shared mailbox delegation
- SharePoint external sharing baseline
- One approved external-sharing pilot site
- Teams guest-access baseline

## Design Decisions
- Shared mailbox permissions are assigned by department role
- Not every mailbox gets the same send rights
- External sharing is allowed only where business need is demonstrated
- One site is approved for external collaboration pilot
- Other department sites remain internal-only

## Business Value
This reduces accidental oversharing, keeps mailbox access intentional, and creates evidence that collaboration controls were applied deliberately.
