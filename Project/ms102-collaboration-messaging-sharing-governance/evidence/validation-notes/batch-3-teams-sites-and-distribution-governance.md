# Batch 3 Validation - Teams, Sites, and Distribution Governance

## Build Scope
This batch created department Teams, created department SharePoint communication sites, assigned business owners to those collaboration resources, and completed the member baseline for the All Staff Updates distribution group.

## Business Outcome
The tenant now has a standardized collaboration structure across Teams, SharePoint, and Exchange. Department collaboration resources are no longer undefined or ownerless.

## Validation Results
- 4 private department Teams were created
- each Team was assigned the intended department owner
- 4 department SharePoint communication sites were created
- each site was assigned the intended department owner
- the All Staff Updates distribution group was populated with the pilot users
- resource ownership is now aligned to business function
