# Batch 4 Validation - Mailbox Delegation and External Sharing

## Build Scope
This batch configured shared mailbox delegation, established a controlled SharePoint external sharing baseline, enabled Teams guest access for the tenant, and validated a single approved external-sharing pilot path.

## Business Outcome
The tenant now has a collaboration governance model where mailbox access is intentional, external sharing is limited to approved scope, and guest collaboration is enabled only through a controlled pilot.

## Validation Results
- Department mailbox delegation was assigned according to role and business need
- Finance Shared and Sales Shared received Full Access plus Send As for their business owners
- Operations Shared and HR Shared received Full Access without Send As
- SharePoint organization-level sharing was configured to support a restricted pilot
- Sales Site was approved for external guest sharing
- Finance, Operations, and HR sites remained internal-only
- Teams guest access was enabled for future governed guest collaboration
- A single external guest invitation path was validated for Sales Site
